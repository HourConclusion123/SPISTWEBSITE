About SPIST

About Us

The Southern Philippines Institute of Science and Technology is dedicated to shaping youth into skilled, values-driven individuals through quality education, hands-on training, and faith-based development. We empower students to realize their God-given potential, equipping them with knowledge, social responsibility, and competitive skills for a progressive, industrializing world. Our mission is to cultivate capable, principled leaders for a meaningful and productive society.

Core Values

To nurture and develop individuals through a technology-integrated skills for a meaningful, productive, and God-fearing society.

Our Mission

To nurture and develop individuals through a technology-integrated skills for a meaningful, productive, and God-fearing society.

Our Vision

To be a builder of youth, highly competitive to meet the growing demands of industrialization in the process.

Senior High School Strands

STEM

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

Dive deep into Advanced Math, Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, and explore Engineering Design, Computer Science, and Environmental Science. Prepare for global challenges like climate change and health while honing critical thinking, problem-solving, and creativity.

ABM

Accountancy, Business, and Management

Learn real-world economics, business ethics, and financial management. Develop leadership skills, explore marketing, and master business math for forecasting and budgeting to prepare for the business world.

HUMSS

Humanities and Social Sciences

Explore human behavior, culture, and politics through subjects like World Religions, Social Sciences, and Literature. Strengthen writing, critical thinking, and empathy, preparing you to become a future changemaker.

TVL

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood

Master food and drink service, cookery, and baking techniques. Learn food safety and kitchen operations, and get ready for TESDA certifications, preparing you for a career in the growing food and hospitality industry.

ICT

Information and Communications Technology

Gain skills in Computer Programming, Web Development, and Network Security. Learn Database Management, Hardware Troubleshooting, and System Administration to prepare for tech-driven industries.
